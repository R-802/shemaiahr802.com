Atlas

Altas is a general purpose virtual assistant, built with OpenAi's Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3 (GPT-3) and Amazon's Polly neural text to speech engine.

Atlas is designed to be an alternative to mainstream AI smart home devices, and ultimately as a platform to build my skillset in this area of programming.

Built with Windows 11 functionality in mind, Atlas is aimed to be seamlessly integrated into a users workflow, assisting them with tasks in their everyday life.
